Mr. Schwab has been Chairman of the Board and a director of CSC since its incorporation in 1986. He also served as Chief Executive Officer of CSC from 1986 to 1997 and as Co-Chief Executive Officer from 1998 until 2003. He was re-appointed Chief Executive Officer in 2004 and served in that role until 2008. He served as Chairman of the Board and a director of CS&#38;Co until 2018. Mr. Schwab is also Chairman of CSB. 

Mr. Bettinger has been President and Chief Executive Officer of CSC since 2008. He serves on the Board of Directors of CSC and CSB, and is Chairman of CS&#38;Co, as well as Chairman and trustee of The Charles Schwab Family of Funds, Schwab Investments, Schwab Capital Trust, Schwab Annuity Portfolios, Laudus Trust, and Schwab Strategic Trust, all registered investment companies and affiliates of CSC. Mr. Bettinger served as President and Chief Operating Officer of CSC from 2007 until 2008 and as Executive Vice President and President &#8211; Schwab Investor Services of CSC and CS&#38;Co from 2005 to 2007. Mr. Bettinger joined Schwab in 1995. 

Mr. Clark has been Executive Vice President &#8211; Advisor Services of CSC since 2012. Mr. Clark has served as Executive Vice President &#8211; Advisor Services of CS&#38;Co since 2010. From 2006 until 2010, Mr. Clark served as Senior Vice President &#8211; Schwab Institutional Sales of CS&#38;Co. Mr. Clark joined Schwab in 1998. 

Mr. Craig has been Senior Executive Vice President of CSC and CS&#38;Co since 2018. He served as Executive Vice President &#8211; Client and Marketing Solutions for CSC from 2017 until 2018 and served as Executive Vice President and Chief Marketing Officer of CS&#38;Co from 2012 until 2018. Mr. Craig joined Schwab in 2000. 

Mr. Crawford has been Executive Vice President and Chief Financial Officer of CSC and CS&#38;Co since 2017. Prior to his appointment as Chief Financial Officer, Mr. Crawford was Executive Vice President of Finance from 2015 to 2017. He served as Senior Vice President of Schwab&#8217;s asset management and client solutions organization from 2008 to 2015. He has served on the Board of Directors of CS&#38;Co since 2018. Mr. Crawford joined Schwab in 2001. 

Mr. Martinetto has been Senior Executive Vice President of CSC and CS&#38;Co since 2015, and Chief Operating Officer of CSC and CS&#38;Co since 2018. He served as Chief Financial Officer of CSC and CS&#38;Co from 2007 until 2017, and Executive Vice President of CSC and CS&#38;Co from 2007 until 2015. He also serves on the Board of Directors of CS&#38;Co and CSB. Additionally, Mr. Martinetto is a trustee of The Charles Schwab Family of Funds, Schwab Investments, Schwab Capital Trust, Schwab Annuity Portfolios, Laudus Trust, and Schwab Strategic Trust. Mr. Martinetto joined Schwab in 1997. 

Mr. Morgan was appointed Executive Vice President, General Counsel and Corporate Secretary of CSC in October 2019. He served as Acting Head of Legal Services for CSC from April 2019 to October 2019. Mr. Morgan served as Senior Vice President and Deputy General Counsel of CS&#38;Co from 2009. He was appointed Executive Vice President and General Counsel of CSB in December 2019, served as Senior Vice President and General Counsel of CSB in 2015, and served as General Counsel of CSB from 2009. Mr. Morgan joined Schwab in 1999. 

Mr. Murtagh has been Executive Vice President &#8211; Corporate Risk and Chief Risk Officer since 2012. He served as Senior Vice President and Chief Credit Officer of CS&#38;Co from 2002 until 2012 and of CSC from 2008 until 2012 when he was also Head of Fixed Income Research for Charles Schwab Investment Management. Mr. Murtagh joined Schwab in 2000.
