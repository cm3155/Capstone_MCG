Procedures for Nominations to the SHUSA Board 

&#160; 

On January 30, 2009, SHUSA became a wholly-owned subsidiary of Santander. Immediately following the effective time of the Santander transaction, because Santander is the sole shareholder of all of SHUSA&#8217;s outstanding voting securities, SHUSA's Board no longer has a formal procedure for shareholders to recommend nominees to SHUSA's Board.
