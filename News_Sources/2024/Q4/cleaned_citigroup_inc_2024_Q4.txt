          United Parcel Service and Citigroup said they have cut thousands of supervisor jobs since last year.
                                                                                  
          Among them, James Riggle, 46 years old, said it is tough to get his resume noticed by job applicant-tracking algorithms.
          "The recruiters aren't even having conversations with me," said Riggle, who was laid off earlier this year after
          15 years at Citi, most of them as a manager in operations such as mortgages, settlement services and
          foreclosures.
