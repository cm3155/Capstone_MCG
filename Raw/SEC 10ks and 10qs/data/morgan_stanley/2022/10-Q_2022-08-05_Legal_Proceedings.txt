Residential Mortgage and Credit Crisis Matters 

On April 27, 2022, the Firm filed a motion for summary judgment in Deutsche Bank National Trust Company, as Trustee for the Morgan Stanley ABS Capital I Inc. Trust, Series 2007-NC1 v. Morgan Stanley ABS Capital I, Inc. concerning plaintiff&#8217;s remaining claim.

On May 12, 2022, the parties in Financial Guaranty Insurance Company v. Morgan Stanley ABS Capital I Inc. et al. filed a stipulation of voluntary discontinuance, dismissing the action with prejudice. 

On July 15, 2022, the Firm filed a motion for summary judgment in IKB International S.A. in Liquidation, et al. v. Morgan Stanley, et al. on all remaining claims. 

Antitrust Matter 

On June 30, 2022, a magistrate judge issued a recommendation that the court certify a class in Iowa Public Employees&#8217; Retirement System et al. v. Bank of America Corporation et al. 

Record Keeping Matter 

The Firm has reached agreements in principle with two regulatory agencies&#8212;the SEC for $125 million and the CFTC for $75 million&#8212;to resolve record-keeping related investigations by those agencies relating to business communications on messaging platforms that had not been approved by the Firm.
