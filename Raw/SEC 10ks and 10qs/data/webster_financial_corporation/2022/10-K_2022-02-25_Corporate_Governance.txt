 ITEM 10. DIRECTORS, EXECUTIVE OFFICERS AND CORPORATE GOVERNANCE 

Directors, Executive Officers, and Corporate Governance 

Webster has adopted a Code of Business Conduct and Ethics that applies to all directors, officers, and employees, including its principal executive officer, principal financial officer, and principal accounting officer. Webster has also adopted a Corporate Governance Policy and a charter for each of Board of Directors' standing committees, which include the Audit Committee, Compensation Committee, Nominating and Corporate Governance Committee, Executive Committee, and Risk Committee. Webster's Code of Business Conduct and Ethics, Corporate Governance Policy, and the charters of the Audit, Compensation, and Nominating and Corporate Governance Committees can be found on its internet website (www.wbst.com).
